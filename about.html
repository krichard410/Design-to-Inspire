About

Who We Are

Design to Inspire began back in 2010. Back then our team only had about five members of our team, now our team has expanded to over double that amount. Our team has talented and cool designers that make this company eye-catching for our clients.






Our Mission

Since Design to Inpsire was created, our mission is to provide the best and aesthetic designs for people around the world. We are working towards one goal: design for the needs of our clients. For each project Design to Inpire takes on, we ensure that the final product meets the clients needs, and is unique.







Our Founder

Sandra Gosling is the imaginative and creative mind behind Design to Inspire. She built the company while studying a Graphic Design degree at the Institute of Design in Halifax, Nova Scotia. With the help of a small team, she led the company to the top rated graphic design company in the world. Aside from her accomplishments, Sandra also loves biking, reading, and you can most likely find her at the beach. She currently lives in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, along with her family.
